Precautions & Health Plan

Important stroke-prevention guidance for patients.

01

Control Blood Pressure

High blood pressure is a major stroke trigger. Track BP regularly and follow medical advice.

02

Manage Glucose

Keep blood sugar under control through diet, activity, and medication if prescribed.

03

Stop Smoking

Smoking increases clot formation and damages brain blood vessels.

04

Stay Active

Regular walking or exercise improves circulation, BP, cholesterol, and glucose.

05

Eat Brain-Healthy Foods

Prefer vegetables, fruits, whole grains, lentils, nuts, fish, and low-salt meals.

06

Know FAST Signs

Face drooping, Arm weakness, Speech difficulty, Time to call emergency help.

07

Maintain Weight

Healthy weight reduces hypertension, diabetes, and vascular strain.

08

Follow Medication

Do not stop BP, diabetes, cholesterol, or heart medicines without doctor advice.
