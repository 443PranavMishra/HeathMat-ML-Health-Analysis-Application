Precautions & Sleep Health Plan

Important sleep-care guidance for patients.

01

Keep Fixed Sleep Time

Sleep and wake at nearly the same time every day to stabilize circadian rhythm.

02

Reduce Screen Light

Avoid bright screens 45-60 minutes before bed to support melatonin release.

03

Manage Stress

Try breathing, meditation, journaling, or relaxation before sleep.

04

Stay Active

Daily walking and exercise improve sleep quality, mood, and heart health.

05

Limit Caffeine

Avoid caffeine late in the day because it can delay sleep onset.

06

Watch Apnea Signs

Loud snoring, gasping, daytime sleepiness, and morning headaches need medical review.

07

Improve Sleep Room

Keep the room dark, cool, quiet, and comfortable.

08

Consult Doctor

If insomnia or apnea symptoms continue, consult a sleep specialist.
