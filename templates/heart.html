Precautions & Health Plan

Important heart-health guidance for patients.

01

Check Blood Pressure

Track blood pressure regularly and consult a clinician if it stays elevated.

02

Control Cholesterol

Limit fried foods, trans fats, and processed snacks. Add fiber-rich meals.

03

Exercise Safely

Walk or exercise regularly, but seek medical advice if chest pain appears.

04

Stop Smoking

Smoking damages blood vessels and sharply increases heart attack risk.

05

Healthy Foods

Prefer vegetables, oats, nuts, legumes, fish, fruits, and low-salt meals.

06

Manage Stress

Sleep well, practice breathing, and reduce chronic stress where possible.

07

Know Symptoms

Chest pain, sweating, breathlessness, jaw pain, or left arm pain need urgent care.

08

Follow Medication

Do not stop BP, cholesterol, diabetes, or heart medicines without medical advice.
